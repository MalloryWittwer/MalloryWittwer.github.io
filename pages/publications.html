M. Wittwer, M. Seita | [Paper in press as of Dec 2021]

Crystal orientation mapping by machine learning

We present a method based on a convolutional neural network to perform crystallographic characterization with high throughput from directional reflectance data. Our method complements state-of-the-art techniques, such as EBSD.

M. Wittwer, B. Gaskey, M. Seita | Materials Characterization 174

Self-supervised microstructure segmentation

We demonstrate that automated grain segmentation can be achieved from directional reflectance data and a machine learning model. Our algotithm performs microstructure analysis with high throughput and minimal human supervision.
