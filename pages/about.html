About me

I am an independent, Switzerland-based tech developer passionate about applications of data science, AI, and web technology. I specialize in creating interactive web-based demonstrators, data visualizations, and software tools for researchers. My goal is to help valorize scientific outputs by presenting them in an intuitive format that facilitates communication of technical concepts with the public, collaborators, and the academic community.
