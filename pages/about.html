Background

In 2018, I graduated from EPFL in Materials Science and Engineering. From 2018 to 2021, I was a research associate at NTU in Singapore. I have developed machine learning-based microscopy techniques for metal additive manufacturing. In December 2021, I completed Le Wagon's Full-stack web development Bootcamp in 9 weeks. Since April 2022, I've been working at EPFL's Image Analysis Hub where I support scientists with image analysis and machine learning for image quantification.
