Project list (2 min read)

1 | High-throughput grain orientation mapping by optical microscopy and machine learning

We are designing a high-throughput method for crystal orientation prediction in superalloys, using custom-built convolutional neural networks (CNN). Results are very promising. Our proposed workflow could significantly accelerate the characterization of material libraries for properties screening and to rapidly infer process-structure-property relationships. This could be a game-changer for R&D in metal additive manufacturing, for example, where engineers spend inordinate amounts of time characterizing samples to determine suitable printing parameters. Project expected to complete by July 2021!

2 | MicroPlanet.io – Image-to-image translation sandbox for satellite imagery

I simulated the Earth and some satellites orbiting around it in Blender. Using a python command line, I can request to render images of the planet at a given time of the year, with the correct sun incident angle. I am thinking of using this setup to render a library of simulated satellite images in the optical range and another in the NIR or SAR data. This could be used to train a GAN for image-to-image translation, as a proof-of-concept.

3 | “Float” – Next generation social network

Existing social networks alienate us because they offer a virtually infinite number of content and people to interact with. I have in mind to design the prototype of a completely different social network. There will be no “Like” or “Follow”. Users will have to showcase their creative skills in order to contact each other!
